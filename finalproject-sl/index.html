Doughnut Delights has been serving C-U with the highest quality doughnuts and coffees since 1971. Nestled in downtown Urbana, Doughnut Delights has become a haven for “Gotta have it” breakfast treats. Our doughnuts are baked fresh twice daily and our coffee is brewed every fifteen minutes. If you have never been to C-U’s hidden gem, then you are missing out!
